Discussion: Poor Requirements Management

For this discussion, think of requirements as capabilities that a software product must possess and exhibit to satisfy the need of its intended users to solve a business problem. 

Instructions

Consider the following question. Be prepared to share your thoughts with the class.

How can poor requirements management processes have an adverse impact on a software development project?

Discussion: Data Types

Both structured and unstructured data types may be found within a typical IT infrastructure, and that sensitive data must be protected regardless of its type and format. 

Instructions

Consider the following questions. Prepare to share your thoughts with the class.

  • How would you describe each data type?
  • Can you provide an example of each data type?
  • Would securing structured or unstructured data likely pose the greater challenge to the enterprise? Justify your answer.
