Discussion: Inserting Security into the CI/CD Pipeline

DevSecOps pipelines can pose challenges to a classical approach to security because not all traditional security tasks translate well into modern software development efforts. However, opportunities exist to embed and mature security processes within software pipelines using automation.

Kannon is a small camera company that has recently started developing companion software to enhance the capabilities of their camera offerings. They do not have the organizational resources to perform extremely high levels of rigor throughout their SDLC, due to limited resources and the fact that Kannon’s software is not critical to their business model.

Instructions

Consider the following question. Be prepared to share your thoughts with the class.

  • What security mechanisms or tools would you implement within the CI/CD pipeline to provide some level of functional and security assurance for automatic software deployments?

Discussion: Software Vulnerability Remediation

You are working for a midsized company that primarily develops mobile applications for Android and iOS. Your organization is in the process of expanding from mobile gaming into applications that will store and process sensitive user data such as PII and PHI. A risk assessment based on this new market is driving the business to embrace deployment and operational security hardening for app hosting infrastructure. There are several vulnerability detection methods for OS, network, and software applications. 

Instructions

Consider the following question. Be prepared to share your thoughts with the class.

  • How might you detect "CVE-2023-27524: Insecure Default Configuration in Apache Superset Leads to Remote Code Execution" within your Apache hosting infrastructure? 
  • How, when, and by whom might the vulnerability be discovered?
  • How might this information impact patch management?
